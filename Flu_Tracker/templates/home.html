Welcome to Flu_TrakR

Tracking Influenza Using Twitter

about

Influenza is a contagious illness that attacks the respiratory organs and in doing so claims the lives of many people throughout the world yearly.

In this modern age of connectivity and information sharing, is it possible to make use of social media to track instances of Influenza? This project was set up in order to research this possibility.

The Facts About Influenza

Duration
  • 5 - 7 Days
Symptoms
  • High Temperature
  • Sore Throat
  • Muscle Pains
  • Fatigue
  • Headache
  • Sometimes more serious illness which may be life-threatening
Deaths Annually
  • 200 - 500 (Ireland)
  • 250,000 - 500, 000 (Worldwide)
